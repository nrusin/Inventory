Remove Product

Basic Path

  1. The Inventory Manager selects "View Products".

  2. The Inventory Manager selects on the products.

  3. The Inventory Manager selects "Remove Product".

  4. The software checks that the product is not being used by an invoice.

  5. The software checks that the product is not being used by an replenishment.

  6. The software checks that the product is not being used by a transfer.

  7. The software checks that the product is not being used by a PO.

  8. The software checks that the product is not being used by a pick order.

  9. The software removes the product.

  10. The software removes the product from any listing of products.
